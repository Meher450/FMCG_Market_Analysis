# FMCG Market & Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes FMCG sales data to identify revenue-driving categories, regional demand patterns, and pricing and promotion opportunities. The objective is to support data-driven business decisions related to product prioritization, pricing strategy, and operational planning.

## Business Objective
- Identify top-performing product categories and regions
- Evaluate the effectiveness of promotions and discounts
- Analyze pricing and delivery factors impacting sales
- Provide actionable insights to support business planning

## Dataset
- 100,000+ FMCG transaction records
- Multiple product categories across regions
- Key fields include sales, revenue, price, discount, promotion flag, region, and delivery lead time
- Dataset used for academic and analytical purposes

## Analytical Approach
- Data cleaning and validation using Python and SQL
- Exploratory data analysis to identify trends and outliers
- Segmentation by category, region, and promotion type
- KPI analysis including revenue contribution, growth, and promotion lift
- Visualization of insights using Power BI dashboards

## Key Insights
- Top three product categories contributed a significant share of overall revenue
- Certain regions showed high demand but lower promotion coverage
- Moderate discount levels performed better than deep discounts
- Longer delivery lead times negatively impacted sales consistency

## Recommendations
- Prioritize inventory and promotions for top-performing categories
- Optimize discount strategies to avoid revenue erosion
- Improve delivery SLAs in high-demand regions
- Reduce promotion spend on low-ROI products

## Tools Used
- SQL
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib)
- Power BI
- Excel
